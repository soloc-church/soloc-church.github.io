About

The team behind Sunday

SOLOC Media is the group of volunteers who carry the technical side of worship — the audio, the camera that keeps the room connected, the screens that keep everyone on the same page.

New volunteers start with the fundamentals: how to set a clean mix, frame a shot, build a readable slide. Once those are second nature, we trust you to develop your own voice. The rules exist so you know what you're breaking — and why.
